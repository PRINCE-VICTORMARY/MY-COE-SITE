Have questions about Growing Minds Great Ideas? Find answers to commonly asked questions here.

1. What is Growing Minds Great Ideas?

- Growing Minds Great Ideas is a TV and social media program aimed at empowering young minds to creatively tackle societal challenges.

2. Who can participate in the auditions?

- Auditions are open to children between the ages of 8 and 11.

3. How can I submit my audition videos?

- You can submit your audition videos through the Google Form provided or via Telegram to the designated contact.

4.When will the TV show air?

- Stay tuned for updates on the schedule and air times of Growing Minds Great Ideas.

5. How can I get involved in community engagement initiatives?

- Reach out to us through the contact information provided to learn more about community engagement opportunities.
