Overview of the Audition Process

The auditions for Growing Minds, Great Ideas are a platform for young minds to showcase their creativity and innovation. Through a multi-stage process, we identify talented individuals who demonstrate the potential to make a positive impact in their communities. Auditions are open to children between the ages of 8 and 11, and participants will be evaluated based on their ability to present ideas related to sustainable agriculture in a creative and engaging manner.

Eligibility Criteria

To participate in the auditions, children must be between the ages of 8 and 11. We welcome individuals from diverse backgrounds and encourage all eligible candidates to apply.

Instructions for Submitting Recorded Videos

Participants can upload up to 10 videos, with each video not exceeding 90 seconds and 100MB in size. To submit your videos, please use one of the following methods:

1. Fill out the Google Form provided here.

2. Send videos via Telegram to +2348034506389 (Charles).

Tips for Recording Your First Set of Video Contents

- Be clear and concise in presenting your ideas.

- Showcase your creativity through various mediums such as poetry, music, drama, or art.

- Ensure that your videos are of high quality and clearly demonstrate your passion and enthusiasm for sustainable agriculture.

By participating in the auditions, children have the opportunity to unleash their potential and become catalysts for positive change in their communities. We look forward to receiving your submissions and witnessing the incredible ideas that will emerge from this process.

Instructions for Video Uploads

Participants are invited to upload their creative submissions showcasing their talents and ideas for sustainable agriculture. Creativity and stage presentation will be judged, so participants should feel free to express their ideas through poetry, songs, drama, comedy, painting exhibition, lone debate, or any other creative initiative. Remember, it's not about perfection; it's about showcasing unique perspective, the ingenuity and passion for making a difference.

Participants can upload up to 10 video contents showcasing their talents and ideas for sustainable agriculture. Each video must not exceed 90 seconds and must be less than 100MB in size. Please ensure that videos are clear, concise, and engaging. Multiple videos can be uploaded in the designated field.
